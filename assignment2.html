Controls

  • Left Click + Drag: Rotate (horizontal = Y-axis, vertical = Z-axis)
  • Right Click + Drag: Pan camera (X-Z plane)
  • Mouse Wheel: Zoom in/out
  • Sliders: Fine-tune rotation and zoom
  • Height: Adjust terrain elevation scale
  • Projection: Switch perspective/orthographic
  • Wireframe: Toggle wireframe view
